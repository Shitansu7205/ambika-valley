Welcome To Ambica Valley Resort | A Nature Retreat in Dang

Discover comfort, tranquility, and natural beauty at Ambica Valley Resort, nestled amidst the scenic landscapes of Dang. Guests can enjoy delicious local and multi-cuisine dishes, unwind in peaceful surroundings, and experience warm hospitality throughout their visit.

Whether you are planning a family vacation, a romantic getaway, a corporate retreat, or simply seeking a peaceful escape from city life, Ambica Valley Resort provides the perfect setting. Explore the beauty of nature, rejuvenate your senses, and create unforgettable memories with us.
