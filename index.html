Elegant Accommodation

Experience comfort and elegance at Gulab Kothi by Grapevine, Varanasi. Our thoughtfully designed rooms offer modern amenities, cozy interiors, comfortable bedding, air conditioning, and complimentary Wi-Fi, ensuring a relaxing stay for leisure and business travelers alike.

Heritage Ambience

Immerse yourself in the timeless charm of Varanasi while enjoying the serene and sophisticated atmosphere of Gulab Kothi. The property beautifully blends traditional hospitality with contemporary comfort, creating a memorable experience for every guest.

Prime Varanasi Location

Conveniently located near Varanasi’s major attractions, Gulab Kothi provides easy access to the city’s spiritual landmarks, cultural heritage sites, local markets, and the iconic ghats, making it an ideal choice for travelers seeking both comfort and connectivity.
