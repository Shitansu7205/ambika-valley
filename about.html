Welcome To Ambika Valley Resort

Experience Nature & Comfort

Your Peaceful Retreat in the Heart of Dang

Ambika Valley Resort offers a comfortable and welcoming stay amidst the serene beauty of Dang. Surrounded by lush greenery and scenic landscapes, the resort provides the perfect escape for travelers seeking relaxation and tranquility. Our well-appointed rooms are equipped with modern amenities to ensure a pleasant and convenient stay.

Guests can savor delicious local and multi-cuisine dishes at our restaurant or unwind in the peaceful outdoor spaces. Conveniently located near popular attractions and natural sites, Ambika Valley Resort serves as an ideal base for exploring the beauty of the region. Whether you're visiting for leisure, family vacations, events, or business purposes, we promise a memorable and refreshing experience.
